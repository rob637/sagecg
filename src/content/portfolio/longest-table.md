## The idea

A "longest table" is exactly what it sounds like: one long table down a closed street, a park path, or a parish hall. Everyone is invited. Nobody sits with only the people they came with.

## What we provide

- Organizer playbooks (permits, food safety, sponsorships)
- A signup and seating engine that intentionally mixes guests
- Templates for sponsors, churches, schools, and city programs

## Why it matters

Loneliness and civic distrust are downstream of how rarely we share a meal with someone we didn't already know. The software is small; the outcome is not.
