## The problem

Football is the most popular sport in America and one of the least understood. Most fans can name the players but can't read the field — why a coverage rotates, how a play-call sets up the next one, when to run and when to throw. That knowledge lives with coaches and film-study diehards, locked behind jargon and hours of tape.

Gridiron X&O turns that knowledge into a game. It's a strategic, chalkboard-style curriculum that teaches the sport one lesson at a time — taking a casual fan all the way to genuine play-calling mastery.

## What we ship

- **A campaign map** — a visual progression tree that gates lessons and walks players from fundamentals up to advanced strategy.
- **ChalkTalks & Coach Identity film rooms** — slide-based reading lessons that teach concepts the way a coordinator would.
- **Drill levels** — rapid-fire repetition minigames that turn recognition into reflex.
- **Scrimmage & drive scenarios** — a deterministic run/pass game engine where you call plays against an AI defense.
- **Shareable Coach Cards** — public player profiles at their own `/u/[handle]` to show off progress.
- **Freemium curriculum** — early units are free, with a paywall unlocking the full path.

## What's coming

- **GM Mode** and **Head Coach** — already wired into the campaign map as visible "coming soon" nodes, previewing the next tiers of the curriculum natively in-app.
