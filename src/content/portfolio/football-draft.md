## What it does

A focused tool for one moment of the year: your league's draft. Real-time tier boards, value-over-replacement, league-specific scoring, and the kind of UI that doesn't get in the way when you're on the clock.

## What it isn't

- Not a year-round fantasy platform
- Not stuffed with ads or affiliate links
- Not a content farm

## Roadmap

- Auction draft mode
- Dynasty and keeper support
