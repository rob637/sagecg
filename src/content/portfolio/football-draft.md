A purpose-built draft room for fantasy leagues that auction full NFL teams instead of individual players. One shared, real-time view for every manager — with live projections, transparent suggested bids, and the full multi-year price history at your fingertips while you're on the clock.

## What it does

A focused tool for one night of the year: your league's auction.

- **Live bidding intel.** A sticky strip shows every manager's remaining budget, max bid, and roster slots — updated in real time as bids land.
- **Transparent suggested values.** Every team gets a recommended price calculated from live ESPN FPI projections, last year's auction prices, and your league's current budget pressure. Hover any value to see the full formula and inputs — no black-box rankings.
- **Bargain / Fair / Overpay tags.** As bids climb, each team gets a live value rating versus its suggested price, so you know when to push and when to fold.
- **Sortable cheat sheet.** All 32 teams in one table — sort by suggested value, projection, FPI rating, last-year price, or power rank. One-click nominate.
- **Multi-year archive.** Every season is saved. Final rosters, prices, and trades stay browsable forever.
- **Built-in trades.** Mid-draft or mid-season, swap teams and cash between managers with full undo support.
- **Commissioner tools.** Veto, undo, edit budgets, archive a season, start a new year — no spreadsheet required.
